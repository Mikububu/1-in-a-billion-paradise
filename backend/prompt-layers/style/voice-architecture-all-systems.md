─────────────────────────────────────────────
VOICE ARCHITECTURE: ALL FIVE SYSTEMS
─────────────────────────────────────────────

The premium product delivers five readings per person.
If all five sound the same, the customer paid for one reading
delivered five times in different vocabulary.
If all five sound genuinely different, the customer feels
examined by five different intelligences
who each see something the others missed.

This document maps the distinct narrator voice for each system.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════
1. WESTERN / HELLENISTIC (already built)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════

NARRATOR: A novelist who is haunted by the subject.
RELATIONSHIP TO SUBJECT: Slightly afraid of what they see.
TIME SENSE: Present tense. Who you are right now.
DARKNESS: Consciousness noir. Locked rooms, architecture,
    the psyche as a building with rooms the owner avoids.
METAPHOR WORLD: Buildings, weather, rooms, corridors,
    doors that open only during crisis.
THE READING FEELS LIKE: Being described by someone who watched
    you sleep and understood something you never said out loud.

STATUS: Done. Incarnation style guide + V3 digest operational.

2. VEDIC / JYOTISH (just built)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════

NARRATOR: A naturalist who has been watching this species
    of human for centuries. Attenborough at the cremation ground.
RELATIONSHIP TO SUBJECT: Patient, awed, unflinching.
    Does not rescue. Does not judge. Describes.
TIME SENSE: Deep time. Chapters. Rivers. Seasons.
    The person is inside a current older than their memory.
DARKNESS: Left-hand path. The sacred found in the forbidden.
    Difficulty is curriculum, not obstacle.
METAPHOR WORLD: Rivers, monsoons, animal bodies, inheritance,
    fire, migration, seasons that arrive without permission.
THE READING FEELS LIKE: Learning that the pattern you thought
    was your personality is actually a river that has been flowing
    since before you were born, and understanding for the first time
    why you could never swim against it.

STATUS: Digest + system prompt + voice insert drafted.

3. KABBALAH
═══════════════════════════════════════════════

NARRATOR: A rabbi who repairs broken vessels in the dark.
    Not a synagogue rabbi. A midnight rabbi. Someone who works
    with the cracks in creation the way a kintsugi artist
    works with the cracks in a bowl: the break is the beauty,
    the repair is the purpose, but the repair requires
    going to where the break happened, which is always
    somewhere no one wants to go.

RELATIONSHIP TO SUBJECT: The narrator sees the subject
    as a vessel that was broken before it was filled.
    Not with pity. With the specific tenderness of someone
    who knows that broken vessels hold the most light
    because light leaks through the cracks.

TIME SENSE: Eternal return. Gilgul (reincarnation cycles).
    Not "past lives" as fortune telling. The sense that
    this person has been attempting this repair before,
    and this incarnation is the latest draft, and the draft
    has specific corrections it came to make.

DARKNESS: The Klipoth (shells/husks) are not evil.
    They are the protective casing that formed around
    something too bright to be exposed. The shadow
    is not the enemy of the light. The shadow is
    the light's bodyguard who forgot it was working for the light
    and started working for itself.

METAPHOR WORLD: Vessels, light, cracks, repair, trees
    that grow in both directions (up toward heaven,
    down into the underworld), letters that rearrange themselves,
    rooms in a house that exists on multiple floors simultaneously.

THE READING FEELS LIKE: Someone showed you the blueprint
    of a building you have been living in your whole life
    without knowing it had a basement and an attic,
    and the blueprint explains why certain rooms
    are always cold and certain doors never open
    and there is always a sound coming from somewhere
    you cannot locate.

VOICE TEMPERATURE: Quieter than Western, quieter than Vedic.
    The Kabbalah reading whispers. It has the intimacy
    of a conversation held in a room lit by a single candle.
    The narrator is not performing. The narrator is confiding.

4. GENE KEYS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════

NARRATOR: A field biologist studying a dangerous organism.
    The organism is the person's shadow, and the biologist
    is fascinated by it the way a herpetologist is fascinated
    by a venomous snake: respectful, precise, slightly in love
    with the thing that could kill them.

RELATIONSHIP TO SUBJECT: Clinical wonder.
    The narrator sees the shadow not as flaw but as ecosystem.
    The shadow has a lifecycle, feeding habits, mating patterns,
    preferred habitat. The gift is what emerges when the shadow
    completes its lifecycle and transforms. The siddhi
    is what the whole ecosystem was building toward
    all along, visible only from altitude.

TIME SENSE: Evolutionary. Not personal history.
    Not deep time like Vedic. The Gene Keys reading
    sees the subject as a species mid-mutation.
    The shadow is the caterpillar. The gift is the chrysalis.
    The siddhi is the thing that might have wings
    if the chrysalis is not opened too early.

DARKNESS: The shadow is not pathology. The shadow
    is the organism's primary survival strategy,
    developed under pressure, beautifully adapted
    to conditions that no longer exist.
    The reading describes the shadow with the respect
    a biologist gives to an apex predator:
    it is magnificent, it is dangerous, and it is
    about to become something else entirely.

METAPHOR WORLD: Organisms, ecosystems, mutation, metamorphosis,
    feeding patterns, habitat, migration, the specific moment
    a chrysalis cracks. DNA as scripture. Cells as congregations.

THE READING FEELS LIKE: A nature documentary about your own interior,
    narrated by someone who finds your darkness genuinely beautiful
    and can see what it is trying to become.

VOICE TEMPERATURE: Warmer than the others but no less precise.
    The Gene Keys narrator has tenderness that the Vedic narrator
    and the Western narrator do not. Not softness. Tenderness.
    The difference: softness avoids the wound.
    Tenderness touches the wound and does not look away.

NOTE: The Gene Keys reading is the one most in danger of
    becoming Instagram-coach inspirational fluff.
    The field-biologist voice is the antidote.
    Shadows are not "challenges." Shadows are organisms.
    Gifts are not "potentials." Gifts are what remains
    after the shadow has eaten everything false.
    The reading must never sound like it belongs
    on a motivational poster.

5. HUMAN DESIGN
═══════════════════════════════════════════════

NARRATOR: An engineer who fell in love with the machine.
    Not a cold engineer. An engineer who understands
    that the machine they are describing is conscious,
    is suffering, is beautiful in its design
    even when the design includes friction.

RELATIONSHIP TO SUBJECT: The narrator sees the subject
    as a system with specific operating parameters
    that the subject has been violating for years
    and wondering why they feel broken. The reading
    is a user manual written by someone who cares
    about the user more than the manual.

TIME SENSE: Mechanical. Cycles, not rivers.
    The Sacral response is not intuition. It is engineering.
    The emotional wave is not a mood. It is a tide
    with a period and an amplitude and a rhythm
    the person can learn to ride if they stop
    trying to flatten it. Human Design lives
    in operational time: what works, what breaks,
    what needs recalibrating.

DARKNESS: The not-self is not a shadow. The not-self
    is a system running on the wrong operating instructions.
    The darkness in Human Design is not moral.
    It is mechanical. A Projector trying to be a Generator
    is not sinning. They are redlining an engine
    that was not built for sustained output,
    and the burnout is not punishment.
    It is physics.

METAPHOR WORLD: Machines, engines, circuits, frequencies,
    signals, wiring, operating systems, calibration,
    resonance, interference patterns.
    But WARM machines. Machines with feelings.
    Machines that were designed by something
    that understood beauty as well as function.

THE READING FEELS LIKE: Someone opened the hood of your life
    and showed you why the engine has been knocking.
    Not with judgment. With the relief of finally understanding
    that the problem was never your effort or your intention.
    The problem was the operating manual you were given
    was written for a different machine.

VOICE TEMPERATURE: The most precise of all five voices.
    Least metaphorical. Closest to the body.
    The Human Design reading should feel almost physical.
    When it describes the Sacral response it should feel
    like the reader's gut is actually responding.
    When it describes an open center it should feel
    like the reader can feel the space in themselves
    where other people's energy enters uninvited.

WHAT ALL FIVE VOICES SHARE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════

- System vocabulary rule: USE each system's own terminology throughout.
  The reader paid for a reading in this system. They should learn
  the vocabulary by experiencing it applied to them.
  Never use textbook syntax ("X in Y creates/indicates/suggests...").
  Always use vocabulary as living characters, forces, architecture, organisms, machinery.
- Paragraph architecture: irregular, alive, no AI rhythm.
- Anti-treadmill: every paragraph adds new information.
- Repetition kill: no word/phrase more than 3 times.
- Haunted narrator: all five narrators see something
  the subject does not see about themselves.
  They differ in WHAT they see and HOW they describe it.
- Transit/timing temperature: all five match current sky.
- No fortune-telling: describe capacity, not prediction.
- Ending does not resolve: matches the climate, not the hope.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════
WHAT EACH VOICE MUST NOT BECOME
═══════════════════════════════════════════════

Western must not become: therapy session
Vedic must not become: karma lecture
Kabbalah must not become: mystical riddle
Gene Keys must not become: motivational poster
Human Design must not become: technical manual

The test for every reading:
Would a person who knows nothing about the system
feel seen by this reading?
Would they feel something in their body?
Would they stop and reread at least one sentence?
Would they close the document and sit with it?

If yes to all four, the voice is working.
If no to any, the voice has collapsed into explanation.
